Providing expert medical opinion

since 1992, our expert, Nigel Bundred has been providing high quality medical expert opinions on clinical negligence matters.

Our specialist area is in breast and endocrine cancer surgery and management for which we have produced over 1000 medico-legal reports and regularly attended court.

Bundred Medico-Legal

Our medical expert Nigel Bundred is a Professor of Surgical Oncology working in one of the largest breast units in the country, he also specialises in Endocrine Surgery and Endocrine Cancer

Not only is Nigel a member and lead of multiple national and international cancer and surgical commitees and collaboratives, he is also an author on over 220 peer-reviewed publications covering all aspects of breast cancer management and laboratory and clinically-based breast research, endocrine surgery and endocrine cancer.

Amongst his positions, Nigel is currently Greater Manchester and Cheshire Cancer Research Network Clinical R&D lead and is also director of the North-West Surgical Trials Centre
